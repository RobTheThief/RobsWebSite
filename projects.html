ROBULATOR




After learning the basics of CSS, HTML and JavaScript I decided that my first project, as it is for many, should be a calculator. The only framework used was TailWindCSS.

It has 2 modes: Algebra and Robulator mode. In Algebra mode, which is the default and the standard for most calculators, multiplication and division is done before addition and subtraction.

Robulator mode essentially calculats from left to right. The idea was to reflect how you might calculate something in a story. E.g. I had 4 biscuits and then Mom gave me 2 more, but Tom stole 3. Harry then said that if I gave him one now he would give me back as many as i had left again. I would then have 4 biscuits. 4+2-3-1*2 = 4. In Algebra mode the answer would be 1.
