I have things to say. Oh how I have things to say. Let me count
the things that I have to say. essop was a stewardess on ocean liners who
began her career aboard the Orinoco in 1908 at age 21.

She started with the White Star Line when she went aboard the HMS Olympic,
one of three Olympic class cruisers created by the company, in 1910.

A year later, while Jessop was still working aboard the ship, it collided with
the British warship the HMS Hawke while the two were passing through a narrow
strait. Though both vessels were damaged by the encounter, it did not
completely destroy either ship and there were no fatalities.











While the Olympic was being repaired, Violet was employed aboard another
White Star Line ship, and the sister vessel to the Olympic, the RMS Titanic.
Jessop was onboard when the Titanic struck an iceberg and sank, but was able
to find a lifeboat and survived.
